Municipal Treasurer's Office

Tax collection, fee payments, and financial services for Solano residents

Office

Municipal Treasurer

Location

Municipal Hall

Hours

8AM - 5PM

Days

Monday - Friday

Real Property Tax Payment

Annual tax payment for land, buildings, and improvements

Requirements

  • Tax Declaration / Assessment
  • Previous Official Receipt (if renewal)
  • Valid ID of Property Owner
  • Authorization Letter (if representative)

Payment Details

  • Fee: Based on assessed value
  • Discount: 10% if paid in January
  • Penalty: 2% per month for late payment
  • Processing: Same day

Community Tax Certificate (Cedula)

Issuance of cedula for individuals and corporations

Individual Cedula

  • Valid ID
  • Proof of Income (if employed)
  • Basic Fee: ₱5.00
  • Additional: ₱1.00 per ₱1,000 income

Corporate Cedula

  • SEC/DTI Registration
  • Business Permit
  • Basic Fee: ₱500.00
  • Additional: Based on gross receipts

Payment Deadlines & Discounts

Important dates for tax payments

Payment Period Deadline Discount/Penalty Notes
Annual (Full Payment) January 31 10% Discount Best option for savings
1st Quarter March 31 No discount/penalty 25% of annual tax
2nd Quarter June 30 No discount/penalty 25% of annual tax
3rd Quarter September 30 No discount/penalty 25% of annual tax
4th Quarter December 31 No discount/penalty 25% of annual tax
Late Payment After deadline 2% per month Maximum 72% (36 months)
