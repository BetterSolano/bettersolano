Issuance of Zoning Clearance/Certification

Required for business permit applications and land use verification

Requirements

  • Duly accomplished Application Form
  • Photocopy of Tax Declaration/Title
  • Photocopy of Latest Real Property Tax Receipt
  • Vicinity Map/Location Plan
  • Barangay Clearance
  • Contract of Lease (if applicable)

Details

  • Fee: ₱100.00 - ₱500.00 (based on floor area)
  • Processing: 1-2 working days
  • Validity: 1 year
  • Who can apply: Property owner or authorized representative

Issuance of Locational Clearance

Required for building permit applications and construction projects

Requirements

  • Duly accomplished Application Form
  • Certified True Copy of Land Title/Tax Declaration
  • Latest Real Property Tax Receipt
  • Site Development Plan
  • Building Plans and Specifications
  • Lot Plan with Technical Description
  • Barangay Clearance
  • Environmental Compliance Certificate (if required)

Details

  • Fee: Based on project cost and floor area
  • Processing: 2-3 working days
  • Validity: Duration of construction
  • For: New construction, renovation, expansion
