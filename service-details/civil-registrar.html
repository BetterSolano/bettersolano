About CENOMAR

The Certificate of No Marriage Record (CENOMAR) is a certification issued by PSA stating that a person has no record of marriage. This is commonly required for marriage license applications, visa applications, and other legal purposes.

Requirements

Valid ID of Requestor
PSA Birth Certificate
Authorization Letter (if representative)
ID of Document Owner

Corrections and Amendments

Clerical error corrections and change of first name

About Corrections

The Local Civil Registrar processes petitions for correction of clerical or typographical errors and change of first name in civil registry documents under RA 9048 and RA 10172.

Types of Corrections

Type Filing Fee Processing Time
Clerical Error Correction (RA 9048) ₱1,000 3-6 months
Change of First Name (RA 9048) ₱3,000 3-6 months
Correction of Day/Month of Birth (RA 10172) ₱3,000 3-6 months
Correction of Sex (RA 10172) ₱3,000 3-6 months

General Requirements

Petition Form
PSA Copy of Document
Supporting Documents
Valid IDs
Affidavit of Publication
NBI Clearance (for CFN)

Legitimation

Legal process to legitimize children born out of wedlock

About Legitimation

Legitimation is the legal process by which children born out of wedlock are legitimized by the subsequent marriage of their parents. This is annotated on the child's birth certificate.

Requirements

Affidavit of Legitimation
Marriage Certificate of Parents (PSA)
Birth Certificate of Child (PSA)
Valid IDs of Parents
CENOMAR of Mother (before marriage)

Fees

Service Fee
Legitimation Annotation ₱500

Delayed Registration

Late registration of birth, marriage, or death

About Delayed Registration

Delayed registration is the registration of vital events (birth, marriage, death) beyond the prescribed period. This requires additional documentary requirements and processing.

Types of Delayed Registration

Type Fee Processing Time
Delayed Birth Registration ₱500 1-2 weeks
Delayed Marriage Registration ₱500 1-2 weeks
Delayed Death Registration ₱500 1-2 weeks

Requirements for Delayed Birth Registration

Negative Certification from PSA
Baptismal Certificate
School Records
Affidavit of Two Disinterested Persons
Marriage Certificate of Parents
Valid IDs
