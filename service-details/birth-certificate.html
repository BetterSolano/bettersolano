Service Overview

The Local Civil Registrar (LCR) of Solano provides certified true copies of birth certificates for persons born and registered in Solano, Nueva Vizcaya. This service is available for walk-in requests during office hours.

Step-by-Step Guide

1

Check Eligibility

You can request a birth certificate if you are:

  • The owner of the document
  • Parent/Guardian of the owner
  • Authorized representative with proper authorization letter
2

Prepare Required Documents

Bring the following:

  • Valid government-issued ID of the applicant
  • If representative: Authorization letter with signature of owner and valid ID of both owner and representative
  • Payment (₱150 per copy)
3

Visit the Local Civil Registrar

Go to the Municipal Civil Registrar Office at the Solano Municipal Hall:

  • Office Hours: Monday to Friday, 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM (Lunch break: 12:00 PM - 1:00 PM)
  • Location: 2nd Floor, Municipal Hall, Solano, Nueva Vizcaya
4

Fill Out Request Form

Complete the request form provided by the office. You'll need to provide:

  • Full name of the person on the birth certificate
  • Date of birth
  • Place of birth
  • Parents' full names
  • Purpose of request
5

Pay the Fee

Pay ₱150 for each certified copy at the Municipal Treasurer's Office (same building).

6

Claim Your Certificate

Return to the Civil Registrar with your official receipt. Your certified copy will be released within 15-30 minutes if the record is readily available.

Required Documents Checklist

  • Valid government-issued ID (original + photocopy)
  • Authorization letter (if applying through a representative)
  • ID of both owner and representative (if applicable)
  • Payment: ₱150 cash
