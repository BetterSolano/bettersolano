HR Management Services

Select a service to view the detailed process and requirements

Issuance of Service Record

Official record of employment history for LGU employees

Requirements

  • Duly accomplished request form
  • Valid government-issued ID
  • Authorization letter (if representative)
StepActivityDurationPersonnel
1 Submit request form with requirements 5 minutes HRMO Staff
2 Verification and preparation of service record 15-30 minutes HRMO Staff
3 Review and signing by HRMO Head 5 minutes HRMO Head
4 Release of Service Record 2 minutes HRMO Staff

Processing Information

  • Total processing time: 27-42 minutes
  • Fee: Free for current employees

Issuance of Certificate of Employment

Official certification of current or previous employment status

Requirements

  • Request letter stating purpose
  • Valid government-issued ID
  • Employee ID (for current employees)
StepActivityDurationPersonnel
1 Submit request letter and requirements 5 minutes HRMO Staff
2 Verification of employment records 10 minutes HRMO Staff
3 Preparation and signing of certificate 10 minutes HRMO Head
4 Release of Certificate of Employment 2 minutes HRMO Staff

Processing Information

  • Total processing time: 27 minutes
  • Fee: Free

Issuance of Certificate of Leave Credits

Official statement of accumulated leave credits (vacation and sick leave)

Requirements

  • Request form/letter
  • Employee ID
StepActivityDurationPersonnel
1 Submit request for leave credits certificate 3 minutes HRMO Staff
2 Computation and verification of leave balances 15 minutes HRMO Staff
3 Preparation and signing of certificate 5 minutes HRMO Head
4 Release of Certificate 2 minutes HRMO Staff

Processing Information

  • Total processing time: 25 minutes
  • Fee: Free

Certificate of No Pending Administrative Case

Certification that employee has no pending administrative case

Requirements

  • Request letter stating purpose
  • Valid government-issued ID
  • Employee ID
StepActivityDurationPersonnel
1 Submit request letter and requirements 5 minutes HRMO Staff
2 Verification of administrative case records 10 minutes HRMO Staff
3 Preparation and signing of certificate 10 minutes HRMO Head
4 Release of Certificate 2 minutes HRMO Staff

Processing Information

  • Total processing time: 27 minutes
  • Fee: Free

Application for Leave

Processing of vacation leave, sick leave, and other leave types

Requirements

  • Duly accomplished CS Form No. 6 (Application for Leave)
  • Medical certificate (for sick leave exceeding 5 days)
  • Supporting documents (as applicable)
StepActivityDurationPersonnel
1 Submit accomplished leave application form 5 minutes HRMO Staff
2 Verification of leave credits and computation 10 minutes HRMO Staff
3 Recommendation by Department Head 1 day Department Head
4 Approval by Municipal Mayor 1-2 days Municipal Mayor
5 Recording and release of approved leave 5 minutes HRMO Staff
Leave TypeDescription
Vacation LeaveFor personal matters, rest, and recreation
Sick LeaveFor illness or medical reasons
Maternity Leave105 days for female employees
Paternity Leave7 days for male employees
Special LeaveFor special circumstances as provided by law
