Registration of Live Birth

Registration of birth for children born in Solano, Nueva Vizcaya

Timely Registration (Within 30 days)

  • Certificate of Live Birth (4 copies)
  • Affidavit of Acknowledgment/Admission of Paternity (if illegitimate)
  • Marriage Certificate of parents (if legitimate)
  • Valid IDs of parents

Delayed Registration (After 30 days)

  • Certificate of Live Birth (4 copies)
  • Negative Certification from PSA
  • Affidavit of Delayed Registration
  • Two (2) documentary evidences
  • Valid IDs of parents and informant
ServiceFeeProcessing Time
Timely RegistrationFREE15-30 minutes
Delayed Registration₱100.001-2 weeks (posting required)

Registration of Death

Registration of death for persons who died in Solano, Nueva Vizcaya

Timely Registration (Within 30 days)

  • Certificate of Death (4 copies)
  • Medical Certificate (if attended by physician)
  • Affidavit of Death (if unattended)
  • Valid ID of informant

Delayed Registration (After 30 days)

  • Certificate of Death (4 copies)
  • Negative Certification from PSA
  • Affidavit of Delayed Registration
  • Burial/Cremation Permit
  • Two (2) documentary evidences
ServiceFeeProcessing Time
Timely RegistrationFREE15-30 minutes
Delayed Registration₱100.001-2 weeks (posting required)
Burial Permit₱50.00Same day

Registration of Marriage

Marriage license application and registration of marriage

Marriage License Requirements

  • Application for Marriage License
  • Certificate of No Marriage (CENOMAR) from PSA
  • Birth Certificate (PSA copy)
  • Certificate of Attendance - Pre-Marriage Counseling
  • Community Tax Certificate (Cedula)
  • Valid IDs of both parties
  • Parental Consent (if 18-21 years old)
  • Parental Advice (if 22-25 years old)
  • 1x1 ID photos (4 copies each)

If Previously Married

  • Death Certificate of former spouse, OR
  • Court Decree of Annulment/Nullity
  • Certificate of Finality

If Foreign National

  • Legal Capacity to Contract Marriage from Embassy
  • Valid Passport
  • Birth Certificate (authenticated)

Corrections and Annotations

Petition for correction of clerical errors and change of first name (RA 9048/RA 10172)

RA 9048 - Clerical Error Correction

  • Petition Form
  • PSA Copy of document to be corrected
  • At least two (2) public documents showing correct entry
  • NBI/Police Clearance
  • Valid IDs

RA 9048 - Change of First Name

  • Petition Form
  • PSA Birth Certificate
  • Newspaper Publication (3 consecutive weeks)
  • NBI/Police Clearance
  • Affidavit of Publication

RA 10172 - Correction of Sex/Day & Month of Birth

  • Petition Form
  • PSA Birth Certificate
  • Medical Certificate (for sex correction)
  • At least four (4) public documents showing correct entry
  • NBI/Police Clearance
  • Newspaper Publication
