# BetterSolano.org

BetterSolano.org is a community-driven platform inspired by [BetterGov.ph](https://bettergov.ph). It strengthens the people of Solano by providing clear and transparent access to local services, public programs, and the responsible use of LGU Solano's funds. It is designed to support an informed, engaged, and empowered community.

**Cost to the People of Solano = P0**

## About

BetterSolano.org is a volunteer-driven civic-tech initiative that aims to modernize and simplify access to government services and data for the Municipality of Solano, Nueva Vizcaya, Philippines. The platform aggregates public information from official government portals and presents it in a user-friendly, accessible format.
